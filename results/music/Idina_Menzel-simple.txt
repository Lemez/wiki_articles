Idina Kim Menzel (born May 30, 1971) is an American actress, singer-songwriter, and singer. She is known for her role as Elsa the Snow Queen in the 2013 movie Frozen and her role as Elphaba in the Original Broadway Production of Wicked, for which she won a Tony Award in 2004. Other notable roles include Maureen Johnson in the original Broadway production of Rent and in the movie based on the play, Shelby Cororan in Glee, and Nancy Tremaine in Disney's Enchanted.
She is currently in the Broadway show If/Then. For her role in that show, she was nominated for a Tony Award.
Menzel was born in Queens, New York.
