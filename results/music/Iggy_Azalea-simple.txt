Amethyst Kelly (born 7 June 1990), better known by her stage name Iggy Azalea, is an Australian rapper and songwriter. She was born in Sydney and raised in Mullumbimby, New South Wales. She started rapping at age 16 when she moved to the United States. In September 2011 she released her first mixtape Ignorant Art. It made two promotional songs, "My World" and "Pu$$y". Her first album, The New Classic, was going to be released in September 2013. It was later released in 2014.
