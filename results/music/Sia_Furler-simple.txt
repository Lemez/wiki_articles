Sia Furler (born 18 December 1975) is an Australian singer. She was born and raised in Adelaide. She is known for her songs: "Clap Your Hands", "Wild Ones" with Flo Rida, "Titanium" with David Guetta and "Chandelier". She also co-wrote "Diamonds" for Rihanna. She wrote "Pretty Hurts", the first song on Beyoncé Knowles' fifth album Beyoncé with Knowles and Ammo. Furler's fifth album We Are Born was released in 2010.
Sia is openly bisexual. She married Erik Anders Lang in August 2014. She has been diagnosed with bipolar II disorder.
