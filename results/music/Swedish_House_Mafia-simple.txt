Swedish House Mafia was a Swedish electronic dance music trio. It was made up of three people: Axwell, Steve Angello and Sebastian Ingrosso. They are all DJs and producers. The band was formed in 2008. In 2012 they said on their website that they will split up at the end of their 2012-2013 tour.
Over the years, Swedish House Mafia have published two albums and six singles. All of these have been very successful worldwide. Five of their singles reached the top ten in the UK music charts. They have worked with many other artists, like Tinie Tempah.
