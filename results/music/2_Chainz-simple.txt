Taheed Epps (born September 12, 1977), better known by his stage name 2 Chainz or Tity Boi, is an American rapper. He is from Atlanta. He's best known for his singles "Riot", "No Lie" (featuring Drake), "I'm Different" and "Birthday Song". He's also known for being half the duo of the rap group Playaz Circle along with rapper and long time friend Dolla Boy. The group was discovered by fellow rapper Ludacris and signed them to his Record Label DTP. 2 Chainz gained recognition as a solo artist when he was featured on Kanye West's 2012 single "Mercy" of his album Cruel Summer. He has 2 albums: Based On A Tru Story and his most recent one B.O.A.T.S: Me Time. He is in the studio working on his third studio album and has released several mixtapes solo.
