Kesha (stylized as Ke$ha; real name: Kesha Rose Sebert, born March 1, 1987) is an American singer-songwriter.


She was born in Los Angeles, California and moved to Nashville, Tennessee in 1991.
In 2005, Kesha was in an episode of The Simple Life.
Her first single "Tik Tok" was number one in the US, Australia, and Canada. She has sold 55 million records.
In 2010, Kesha's first album Animal was released. Her first extended play Cannibal was released later that year.
Kesha is openly bisexual. In January 2014, she entered rehab for treatment for an eating disorder. Her mother says that her disorder started when she was only 18.
