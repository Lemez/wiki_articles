Alecia Beth Moore, better known as Pink, (born September 8, 1979) is an American singer-songwriter and actress. She sings pop rock, pop and R&B. As of 2012, she sold over 40 million records and 70 million singles worldwide. She has won three Grammy Awards and a Brit Award.
In 2006, she married Carey Hart in Costa Rica. On June 2, 2011, they had a baby girl named Willow Sage Hart. Willow is the first child for both Pink and Hart.
